FOOD

Misokatsu

Deep fried pork cutlets served with a thick, miso sauce, shredded cabbage, and rice.

Hitsumabushi

Finely chopped grilled eel with a soy sauce based glaze serve on top of rice in a wooden tub.

Miso Nikomi Udon

Udon simmered in soybean paste and bonito stock usually topped with eggs, chicken, and fish cake.

Tenmusu

A rice ball filled with a flavoured small shrimp tempura wrapped with seaweed. It makes a great snack.

Tebaski

Deep fried chicken wings that are salty, sweet, and spicy that pair great with cold beer.
