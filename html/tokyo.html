TOKYO

ATTRACTIONS

Shibuya

There are lots of shopping malls and departments stores in Shibuya. The area is known for its busiest intersection in Japan - Shibuya crossing.

Tokyo Skytree

An observation and broadcasting tower. It is the tallest tower in Japan at 634 meters tall. There are 2 observation desks for visitors.

More Info

Tokyo Tower

An observation and communication tower. It is the second tallest tower in Japan at 332.9 meters tall. It is a popular spot to take photos.

More Info

Tokyo Disney Resort

Opened in 1983, it is the first Disney theme park outside of the US. It also has DisneySea where it highlights world-famous harbours.

FOOD

Tendon

Shortened from Tempura Donburi, this dish consists of a bowl of rice topped with deep-fried tempura.

Tsukemen

Cold ramen noodles dipped in a hot soup broth. Usually eaten with a ramen egg and other toppings.

Omurice

A fluffy, yellow omelet placed ontop of rice. Usually eaten with a demi-glaze sauce over it or beef curry.

Anago

Saltwater conger eel, normally referred to as ma-ango. Often simmered as sushi or deep-fried into a tempura.
