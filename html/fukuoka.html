Kushida Shrine

Built in 757, this shrine is shaded by a 1000 year old ginko tree. Summer festivals Hakata Gion Yamakasa and Hakata Okunchi start at this shrine.

The castle ruins lie in the centre of Fukuoka. The best time to visit is between late March and early April when cherry blossoms bloom.

Food

Hakata Ramen

Aka Tonkatsu Ramen, it is named after the old name for the region. This dish consists of a pork broth with straight, thin egg noodles.

Mentaiko

Spicy salted pollack roe that you can eat over rice with some sake or beer. You can also use it for cooking or as a side dish for your meals.

Motsunabe

A hot pot dish that consists of beef or pork, cabbage, garlic, chives, togarashi peppers in a soy suace or miso based soup served with rice.

Tetsunabe Gyoza

Pan fried dumplings served in a cast iron skillet that are typically stuffed with a mixture of ground pork, cabbage, and green onions.
